About

The Story Behind the Platform

I'm Prakash Choudhary — an AI educator, digital builder, and Web3 advocate with a passion for making complex technology accessible to everyone. My journey began with a simple belief: that the most transformative technologies of our time should not be the exclusive domain of engineers in well-funded tech hubs.

From rural communities in India to global online audiences, I've worked to bridge the knowledge gap between where technology is developing and where it needs to go. Whether it's helping a farmer understand how AI can transform their crops, a teacher integrate AI tools into their classroom, or a professional build their digital presence — the goal is always the same: practical knowledge that creates real impact.

Prakash Knowledge Hub is the platform I built to scale that mission. Every article is written to be immediately useful — not just theoretically interesting. I believe in learning by doing, building with purpose, growing with intention, and creating impact that outlasts any single technology trend.
